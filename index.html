【中文全文】我的家乡萍乡坐落于江西西部，群山环绕，自然风光秀美，本地人淳朴实在。萍乡最出名的便是本地特色辣椒，和别处辣椒截然不同，凑近就能闻到浓郁诱人的香气，辣味醇厚。街边小吃都离不开这款辣椒，漫步老城街巷，一年四季都能飘着诱人的香辣气息。除却特色美食，这里闲适安逸的生活氛围，总能让我心生暖意。

【English full text】Pingxiang is my lovely hometown located in western Jiangxi Province. Surrounded by rolling green mountains, the city boasts beautiful countryside scenery and down-to-earth local residents. What makes Pingxiang stand out most is its distinctive chili peppers. Unlike chilies grown in other regions, local Pingxiang peppers carry an inviting, rich fragrance the moment you get close to them, long before you taste their spicy kick. Street-side snack shops rely heavily on these special peppers to cook classic local dishes. Wandering around old downtown alleys, the tempting spicy aroma drifts through the air all year round. Apart from iconic spicy food, the town keeps a slow, peaceful lifestyle that always makes me feel warm and at home.
